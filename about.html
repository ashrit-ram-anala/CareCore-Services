About Us

Our Mission

Our organization’s main focus is preventive care, which is healthcare that helps prevent health problems or finds them before they become much more serious. This is accomplished through health screenings, interventions, tests, etc. We believe education regarding preventive care resources is the first and most influential step and we aim to bring those resources to as many under-funded communities as we can.


Our Plan

Through initiatives like informative articles and infographics, we can make a difference. Since language barriers are a significant obstacle in accessing information, we are committed to making all of our resources available in different languages. As such, we have made this very website available in over 120 languages so this information is kept from no one. Not only do we strive to form an organization of individuals who are invested in our mission, but we also strive to form a community with those we help. Overall, Carecore Services is an organization that believes that prevention is the best treatment, and a treatment that should be available for all.



Seminars

We also plan to have several medical professionals in various fields of healthcare explain the benefits of preventive care and the drastic effects of not utilizing it. Alongside professionals, we plan to have guest speakers recount their own personal stories from various communities to have the value of these resources truly hit home.





Next Steps

Going forward, we plan to continue to expand our reach from a local community level to the global stage. We also plan to set up clinics and create branches across the country and even the world so no person lacks the healthcare information they are entitled to.
